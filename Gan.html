About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer specializing in Chinese language and culture. My primary interest is in understanding how people from different backgrounds communicate with each other. I have a passion for exploring intercultural communication and helping others navigate the complexities of language and cultural exchange.

Work and Projects

Currently, I am working on several research projects that investigate the dynamics of intercultural communication in various contexts. My work aims to bridge cultural gaps and promote mutual understanding through language education and cultural studies.
